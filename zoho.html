DATA INTEGRITY MANDATE

Seamless Migration Roadmap

We follow a strict 4-step data-validation pathway when migrating accounting from Tally, QuickBooks, or Excel templates to Zoho Books.

01

Audit & Extract

Auditing existing journals to identify entries mismatches. Cleaning customer, vendor lists, and inventory master records before exporting historical databases.

02

Schema Design

Mapping custom Charts of Accounts (COA) to fit Zoho's database logic. Configuring tax profiles, TDS categories, multi-currency rates, and opening balances.

03

Import & Validate

Uploading files securely. Validating post-import ledger integrity by reconciling Trial Balances and bank sheets against previous databases.

04

Go-Live Training

Syncing API links with Zoho Inventory/CRM. Conducting hands-on training sessions for your accounting staff to support daily invoicing workflows.
