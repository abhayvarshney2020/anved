Direct Taxation

Corporate & Personal Tax Governance

Our direct taxation practice includes quarterly filing of TDS returns, computation of advance taxes to be paid during the fiscal year, and professional filing of Income Tax Returns (ITR) detailed as under:

Advance Tax Payments

Quarterly tax computation including preparation of provisional computation of income factoring business projections. The objective is to minimize or negate interest exposure on short or late payments.

TDS Quarterly Filing

Preparation & filing of TDS returns in relevant forms within the stipulated timeframe along with issuance of certificates. Advising on WHT (Withholding Tax) requirements, decoding the provisions of law.

Filing of Income Tax Return

Preparation of tax returns based on accounts and other information finalized during the course of Audit. Post year-end, we advise on tax payable/refundable to the Company and provide on-call assistance in e-filing the corporate tax return on the web-portal of the revenue authorities.

Indirect Taxation

GST Review, Compliance & Reconciliation

We review the initial GST registration obtained by your organization to ensure that the particulars are aligned with the actual activity being undertaken. For consecutive GST filings, our assistance comprises:

Returns Finalization

Assisting in finalizing the respective returns based on the accounts and other information on a monthly, quarterly, or annual basis. Further, we advise you of the balance of tax which appears to be payable.

Purchase Registers Reconciliation

Assisting in reconciling the purchase register with auto-populated GSTR-2A/GSTR-2B datasets and calculating the input tax credit (ITC) that may be claimed for specific return periods.

Payment & Filing Support

Providing over-the-call assistance on facilitating payments against the GST liability and filing the respective return forms on the portal.

Applicable GST Forms

GSTR-1: Details of outward supplies.
GSTR-3B: Summary return showing outward supplies, input tax credits, and payment clearances.
GSTR-9: Annual return for normal taxpayers.

Secretarial Services

Company Law & Secretarial Governance

We assist in finalizing and filing the respective returns and statements based on information supplied to us by your company. Our deliverables safeguard you from Company Law deviations:

Board Meetings & Notices

Drafting of the Quarterly Board Notices, Board Meetings resolutions, and providing company secretarial advices on an as-needed basis.

Risk & Deviation Tracking

Highlighting any key risk factor or deviation to the Company on a real-time basis from a Company Law compliance perspective.

Applicable Regulatory Forms

We compile and upload essential forms: DPT-3 (Return of Deposits), MGT-7 (Annual Return), AOC-4 (Annual Financial Statement, Directors & Auditors Reports), DIR-3 KYC (KYC details of Directors), and ADT-1 (Appointment/Re-appointment of Statutory Auditors).

Payroll Processing

End-to-End Payroll Administration

We provide end-to-end assistance in connection with payroll management, covering statutory compliance pertaining to employees' withholding taxes and preparation of tax certificates:

Monthly Payroll Processing

Preparation of monthly pay registers showing employee-wise analysis of basic salary, allowances, deductions (TDS, PF, PT, etc.) & net amount payable. Drafting bank transfer letters, collecting investment declarations/proofs, and issuing Form 16.

Employee Onboarding & Attrition

Updating compensation configurations for new joiners. Fetching previous employer parameters for mid-year joiners to compute correct TDS. Formulating final settlements and recoveries (advances, notice periods) upon attrition.

Advisory Services

Strategic Tax & Exchange Control Advisory

Our advisory services safeguard your transactions under prevailing regulations. We conduct periodic alignment meetings with your finance departments to govern compliance updates:

On-Call Consultations

Routine on-call and detailed advisory services on changing laws, document/agreement reviews, and exchange control regulations (FEMA) affecting daily business transactions.

Governing Acts

We advise across the Income Tax Act, DTAA (Double Tax Avoidance Agreements), GST Legislations (including CGST, IGST, SGST, and Compensation to States), and foreign exchange control guidelines.

Litigation Support

Assessments & Notice Representation

In a scenario where notices are issued by departments or regulatory authorities, we guide your response draftings and represent your case before assessment officers:

Response Drafting & Submissions

Reviewing notices, determining approach, assisting in organizing requested documentation, and drafting technical submissions for authorities.

Tribunal Representation

Representing your company before authorities/Courts and compiling/filing appeals and objections against assessment orders.

Transfer Pricing

Cross-Border Transaction Auditing

Through years of practice, we assist international groups in drafting Transfer Pricing (TP) disclosures to comply with arm's length regulations:

TP Documentation

Verifying inter-company transactions, analyzing comparable databases, implementing FAR (Functions, Assets, Risks) analyses, and applying MAM (Most Appropriate Method) calculations.

Accountant's Report (3CEB)

Auditing international transactions under Section 92E guidelines, drafting and certifying the Accountant's Report in Form 3CEB for portal upload.

Master File Compliance (Form 3CEAA)

Preparing Master Files in Form 3CEAA for entities part of international groups, ensuring statutory thresholds are monitored and CbCR filings are reviewed.

Statutory & Tax Audit

Standards of Auditing & Regulatory Audits

We execute mandatory corporate compliance audits, combining statutory financial audits under the Companies Act and tax audits under the Income Tax Act:

Statutory Audit

Conducted in accordance with Standards of Auditing. Phase I: Risk of Material misstatement identification & internal controls checks. Phase II: Evaluation of statement presentation structures. Phase III: Providing standalone True & Fair audit opinions.

Tax Audit (Section 44AB)

Objectives include ensuring income-expenditure details are captured appropriately. We verify returns accuracy, check for internal frauds/malpractices, and report tax depreciation compliances.

Key Facets of Audit Procedure

Financial Reporting: Preparation of statements giving a true and fair view.
Accounting Records: Maintenance of records for safeguarding assets.
Internal Controls: Devising proper systems to ensure compliance with laws.

Governing cross-border corporate law compliance and strategic investment tax advisor services.
