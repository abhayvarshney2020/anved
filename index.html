Our Delivery Process

We follow a structured 4-step delivery model to ensure quality and compliance on all assignments.

/01

Gathering Information

Gather information/documents to adhere with compliances or execute an assignment. Our info request would be tailor-made to suit company's operation.

/02

Processing the Information

Analysing information shared and seeking clarifications, if needed. Our analysis would ensure that all risk parameters or inconsistencies, if any, are highlighted in a timely manner.

/03

Sharing First Cut

Share soft copies of deliverable with you, ahead of the due date, so that you have sufficient time to review at your end.

/04

Finalizing Deliverable

We would assist in finalization of the deliverable and timely filing of the same during the course of the engagement.
